Sir Isaac Newton

Timeline of Sir Isaac Newton's life

  • 1643 - Born in Lincolnshire, England.
  • 1661 - Admitted to Trinity College, Cambridge.
  • 1665 - Discovered the generalised binomial theorem (calculus).
  • 1667 - Return to Cambridge after the Great Plague.
  • 1687 - Discovery of gravity.
  • 1687 - Principia was published, stating the three universal laws of motion.
  • 1727 - Died in Middlesex, England.
